Services Provided

Expert Consultation & Testimony

Forest Data Works provides expert analysis and testimony for questions about forest resources. Whether you’re a private landowner or represent an NGO, local government, or larger agency, our deep technical knowledge and extensive experience in working with natural resource stakeholders will help you navigate through challenges to a successful conclusion.

Data Analysis

Funding programs and regulatory agencies are increasing their requirements for carbon accounting, growth modeling, forecasting, and site monitoring. We offer analytical services with understandable results to complete these tasks, so you can focus on achieving your vision for the land.

GIS Analysis & Mapping

Organized spatial data products and maps can elegantly illustrate a point or provide a new and useful perspective. We provide Geographic Information System (GIS) analysis and mapping so you can take advantage of these modern tools by putting your resource issue in the context of broader geographical datasets.

Forest Inventory & Assessment

A well-designed sample to gather information will give you the foundation to answer questions and build towards your goals. Whether you are interested in forest management, ecological condition, wildlife management, trails, or other forest attributes, let us help you plan, execute, and summarize your forest inventory so that it serves you now and through time.
